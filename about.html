Our Story & Mission

Pioneering Agricultural Excellence in Malawi

Founded in 2010, Lion Cage Global Farm has grown from a small family operation into one of Malawi's leading agricultural enterprises. Our journey began with a simple mission: to produce the highest quality agricultural products while contributing to sustainable farming practices and economic growth in our community.

Today, we manage over 500 hectares of prime farmland across the Lilongwe region, specializing in both cash crops and livestock production. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and community development has made us a trusted partner for both domestic and international markets.
